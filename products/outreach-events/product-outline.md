# Outreach Events Calendar Product Outline

The VA.gov [Outreach Events calendar](https://www.va.gov/outreach-and-events/events/) has grown (to more than 200,000 page views on average per month [Google Analytics report](https://analytics.google.com/analytics/web/?authuser=1#/report/content-pages/a50123418w177519031p176188361/_u.date00=20190701&_u.date01=20210722&explorer-table.filter=outreach-and-events~2Fevents~2F&explorer-table.plotKeys=%5B%5D&explorer-graphOptions.selected=analytics.nthMonth)) and so have the needs and expectations of the VA business stakeholders.   The Veterans Experience Office has [business users](https://github.com/department-of-veterans-affairs/va.gov-team/blob/master/products/outreach-events/business-users.md) for the national events calendar on digital comms teams at VHA, VBA, OPIA and NCA.

## North Star
Providing a first-class, modern on-site events tool for Veterans that fully aligns with our North Star goals of:
- Increase the use of self-service tools, focusing on services most important to Veterans.
- Consistently deliver a satisfying, reliable, and secure online experience.

## User Problem Statement
As a Veteran, I am easily able to find local VA events, including any relevant information required to attend.

### User Goals
- As a veteran, I want to easily and quickly find the information I need with few clicks as possible on the VA.gov Website.
