## Initiative Kickoff Checklist

Work with team and stakeholders to complete this checklist for all new projects. Capture responses in the [initiative brief]([url](https://github.com/department-of-veterans-affairs/va.gov-team/tree/master/products/health-care/appointments/va-online-scheduling/initiatives)). 

### What do we know?

- [ ] What is the problem statement?
- [ ] Who is the target user(s)?
- [ ] What are our starting questions, assumptions, ideas?
- [ ] What other information exists (existing research, metrics, etc.)?

## Who's involved?

- [ ] Who surfaced the issue/problem/opportunity?
- [ ] Who are the main stakeholders?
- [ ] Who is leading this effort?

## What value does solving this problem (potentially) bring?

- [ ] What value does product, UX, engineers, and/or stakeholders expect this will bring target users?
- [ ] How do we expect to measure success?
