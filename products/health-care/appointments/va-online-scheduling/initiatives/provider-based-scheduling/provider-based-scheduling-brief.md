# Provider Based Scheduling - Product Initiative
---

## Overview
Add Provider based scheduling to the VA online scheduling tool where Veterans are able to more schedule an appointment based on the provider of choice. 

## Problem Statement
As a Veteran who uses VA online scheduling, I want to be self schedule or request an appointment from a provider of my choice. 
 
## Desired Outcomes
- Be able to direct schedule or request an appointment based on a selection of the provider 
- Improve overall satisfaction (rating) of VA online scheduling
- Improve retention rate of VA online scheduling 

## Undesired Outcomes
- 
- Increase in abandonment of online scheduling
