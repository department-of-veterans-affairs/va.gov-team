# Preferred date - Community Care Request Flow

## Overview
Veterans are asked to choose a preferred date for when they'd like to receive care. 

## User stories

- As a Veteran I want to choose when I will receive care.
- As scheduling staff I want Veteran appointments to be scheduled at a time that works for the patient, provider, and is also the appropriate length of time corresponding to the type of appointment.
