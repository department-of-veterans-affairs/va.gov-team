# UAE Feedback URLs

URLs relevant to UAE tools that we request/review Medallia feedback from.

**Legend**
`Sub-URLS?` - Indicates whether or not we want feedback from all SUB-urls under the URL
`Auth or Public` - Indicates whether the user must be logged in (Auth) when viewing the page, or if it can be accessed by anyone (Public)
`CSAT?` - Indicates whether the URL is included in the CSAT calculation in Domo (see Domo dashboard)
