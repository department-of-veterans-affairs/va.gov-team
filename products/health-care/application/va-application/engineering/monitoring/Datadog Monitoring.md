- [10-10EZ logs](https://vagov.ddog-gov.com/logs?query=env%3A%22eks-prod%22%20service%3Avets-api%20%40message_content%3A%2A%22%5BHCA%5D%22%2A&agg_m=count&agg_m_source=base&agg_t=count&cols=host%2Cservice&messageDisplay=inline&refresh_mode=sliding&source=monitor_notif&storage=hot&stream_sort=desc&viz=stream&from_ts=1753018315866&to_ts=1753191115866&live=true) and [HCA logs](https://vagov.ddog-gov.com/logs?query=env%3A%22eks-prod%22%20service%3Avets-api%20%40message_content%3A%2A%22%5BHCA%5D%22%2A&agg_m=count&agg_m_source=base&agg_t=count&cols=host%2Cservice&fromUser=true&messageDisplay=inline&refresh_mode=paused&source=monitor_notif&storage=hot&stream_sort=desc&viz=stream&from_ts=1753018646000&to_ts=1753191446000&live=false) Logs around the 10-10EZ. - [healthcare-application service](https://vagov.ddog-gov.com/apm/traces?query=%40_top_level%3A1%20env%3Aeks-prod%20service%3Ahealthcare-application&agg_m=count&agg_m_source=base&agg_t=count&cols=core_service%2Ccore_resource_name%2Clog_duration%2Clog_http.method%2Clog_http.status_code&fromUser=false&graphType=flamegraph&historicalData=true&messageDisplay=inline&query_translation_version=v0&shouldShowLegend=true&sort=time&spanType=service-entry&spanViewType=errors&storage=hot&traceQuery=&view=spans&start=1753018773381&end=1753191573381&paused=false) Endpoint traces for all of the 10-10EZ endpoints.
