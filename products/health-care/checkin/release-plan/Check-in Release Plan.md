## Phase I: Limited release of MVP

### Planning:
- Desired date: Target launch 7/30/2021
- Desired number of users: MVP site will be Martinsburg, WV. Number of users to be determined based on number of clinics where the MVP is made available, number of daily appointments in those clinics, and number of Veterans who opt into using the MVP. Ballpark/SWAG is 10 users per day.
- How you'll conduct the testing: 
  - See to [Research Plan](https://github.com/department-of-veterans-affairs/va.gov-team/blob/master/products/health-care/checkin/research/veteran-facing/mvp-UAT/research-plan.md) for MVP launch
  
  
### Key Performance Indicators (KPI)
- % of text messages that result in a check-in link being sent
- % of links sent that are clicked on
- % of unique pageviews with at least one click
- % of unique pageviews that reach the end of the flow (either "no" and checked in, or "yes" and ended)
- % of unique pageviews where user selected "No" and proceeded to check in button
- % of unique pageviews where user reached check in button and clicked button
- % of unique pageviews where user encountered an error
- % of unique pageviews where back button was used
- average time from initial text message to end of flow (either checked in or diverted to clerk)
