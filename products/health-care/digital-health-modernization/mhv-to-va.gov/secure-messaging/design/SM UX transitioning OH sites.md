# SM UX during data migration for transitioning OH sites
This doc explains patients' secure messaging UX during the data migration period when their facility transitions to OH. The data migration period is when we'll block users from sending or replying to messages at transitioning facilities. This is to prevent data loss during the migration period. 

You can [review the EHR deployment schedule on this VA page](https://digital.va.gov/ehr-modernization/ehr-deployment-schedule/). Upcoming MI sites in April 2026: 
- VA Battle Creek Medical Center	 
- VA Detroit Healthcare System	
- VA Ann Arbor Healthcare System	
- VA Saginaw Healthcare System
