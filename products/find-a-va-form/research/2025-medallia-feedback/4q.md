## VA.gov Experience score analysis

**Total responses: 267**

### Positive feedback
Number of mentions: 118
- Ease of use
- Easy to find what they're looking for
- Has a lot of helpful information

### Form-related Issues
Number of mentions: 34
- Common issues:
  - Problems with form fields (e.g., can't fill out whole field even with room, not sure how to fill out certain fields)
  - No address given to mail in form 
  - Issue accessing a form 
  - Broken links and non-existent sites when trying to access forms
  
### Website navigation and usability issues
Number of mentions: 46
- Common issues:
  - Difficulty finding specific forms or information
  - Confusing navigation and layout, particularly for older veterans
  - Difficulty finding information for caregivers and family members

### Technical and login issues
Number of mentions: 20
- Common issues:
  - Problems with accessing or logging into the website
  - Issues with verifying identity for online access
  - Website functionalities not working properly (e.g., printing forms, and saving forms)

### General complaints and frustrations
Number of mentions: 49
- Common issues:
  - Too much information
  - Too many steps and process takes too long
  - Perception of the website being designed to be overly complicated
  - Frustration with system inefficiencies and bureaucratic processes

## Task-based feedback

**Total responses:**
- Key themes
  - Forms and submission issues (42)
  - Technical issues with the website (2)
  - General feedback on VA services and processes (9)
  - Travel reimbursement (14)

### Forms and submission issues
This is the most prevalent category. The main pattern indicates a significant need for better instructions, error-free forms, and the ability to submit forms online directly. Respondents frequently express frustration over not knowing where to submit forms, issues with form fields, and the lack of online submission options.
Total responses: 42
- Common issues:
  - Forms not having a clear submission address
  - Inability to upload forms directly to the website
  - Difficulty in downloading or filling out forms
  - Problems with form fields, such as limitations on input (e.g., date fields)
  - Problems with specific forms not working
    
### Technical issues with the website
Total responses: 2
- Issues:
  - Problem printing from the website
 
### General feedback on VA services and processes
Total responses: 9
- Issues:
  - Trying to get info about benefits for spouse
  - Upset about partisan messaging during shutdown
    
### Travel reimbursement
Total responses: 14
- Common issues:
  - Difficulty with submitting travel reimbursement claims; complicated process
  - Lack of clear instructions on how to file for travel reimbursement
  - Process used to be easier
