## Problem Statement
**Painpoint:** Development on the existing forms library has stalled and documentation is severely lacking
**How will we solve the pain point**: Identify and prioritize quick wins that will help to soften the pain for developers building forms for va.gov.

## Desired User Outcomes
- Major developer pain points are outlined
- Prioritized development\documentation backlog is executed on
- Communication channels and feedback loops with VFS\VSA customers are created\maintained
- Research and insight is gathered to [help drive more comprehensive future work](https://app.zenhub.com/workspaces/vsp-5cedc9cce6e3335dc5a49fc4/issues/department-of-veterans-affairs/va.gov-team/4599).  

## Undesired User Outcomes
- Customers (VFS Developers or Designers) feel required to implement their own solutions because VSP is not meeting their needs

## Desired Business Outcomes
- Forms Library Component Development remains in sync with VA Design System
