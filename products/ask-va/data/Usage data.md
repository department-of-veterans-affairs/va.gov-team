### How many business submissions are being submitted per category?
_Date added: Dec 22, 2025_

**Description:** As we break apart the business and personal experiences, we wanted to understand how many inquiries are being submitted by business users in relation to total submissions.

#### Monthly average Business submissions
- **19.49%** -- All categories and topics
   - **18.42%** -- Education benefits and work study 
      - **15.22%** -- Work study
      - **1.35%** ---- School Certifying Officials (SCOs)
      - **1.09%** ---- Post-9/11 GI Bill (Chapter 33)
   - **0.85%** --- Debt for benefit overpayments and health care copay bills
      - **0.81%** ---- Education benefit overpayments (for school officials)
   - **Less than 0.23%** among all other categories and topics

_Date of Data: June-November 2025_

_Data Source: [Custom CRM Report(12-17-2026)](https://dvagov.sharepoint.com/:x:/r/sites/AskVA/Shared%20Documents/General/AVA%20VA.gov%20Team/Research%20%26%20Design/2025%20AVA%20Inquiry%20Report.xlsx?d=wc42f4a5021e248e1baa33ff1c0503dce&csf=1&web=1&e=aLTC92)_

### How many submissions are submitted with attachments?
_Date added: Dec 22, 2025_

**Description:** As we break apart the business and personal experiences, we wanted to understand how many inquiries are being submitted with attachments for both Business and Personal users.

**79.53%** -- of all Business submissions have attachments

**22.59%** -- of all Personal submissions have attachments

**Top 3 Categories Business submitters have submitted with attachments**
- Education Benefits and Work Study
- Debt for Benefit Overpayments and Health Care Copay Bills
- Healthcare

_Date of Data: June-November 2025_
