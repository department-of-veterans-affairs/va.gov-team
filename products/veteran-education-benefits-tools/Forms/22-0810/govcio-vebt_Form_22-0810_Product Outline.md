## Overview
* Currently available as a PDF-only download, the 22-0810 form is used for reimbursement for national exam fees.  Digitizing the form will increase VA efficiencies and streamline and enhance the overall user experience. *
## Problem Statement
*How might we streamline and digitize the Request for Reimbursement For Reimbursement of National Exam Fee form to allow Veterans to submit their request for reimbursement? 
Who: Veterans and beneficiaries 

What: The current Request for Reimbursement of National Exam Fee form exists as a PDF, which limits accessibility and convenience. 

Why: By digitizing the form, we can remove barriers to entry, making it easier for users to submit their forms. This will help improve the reimbursement process. 

Where: The current form is not accessible, restricting potential users who prefer or need a digital solution. Making the form available online will ensure broader and more convenient access. 

## Desired User Outcomes
Why would a user want this? 

To easily submit a reimbursement request for a national exam fee. 

With this problem solved, what should users be able to do/achieve that they couldn’t before?

Seamlessly complete and submit the reimbursement form for national exam fees.
