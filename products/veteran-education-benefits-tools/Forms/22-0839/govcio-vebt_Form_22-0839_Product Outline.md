## Overview
*Currently available as a PDF-only download, the 22-0839 form is used for the Yellow Ribbon Program Agreement.  Digitizing the form will increase VA efficiencies and streamline and enhance the overall user experience.* 

## Problem Statement
*How might we streamline and digitize the Yellow Ribbon Program Agreement to allow domestic and foreign institutions of higher learning (IHLs) to participate in, modify or withdraw from the program?*

Who: Yellow Ribbon Program Point of Contact (POC), School Certifying Official (SCO), and Authorizing Officials. 

What: The current Yellow Ribbon Program agreement exists as a downloadable PDF, which limits accessibiity and convenience. 

Why: By digitizing the form, we can remove barriers to entry, making it easiers for users to submit their agreements. This will help improve efficiencies with the Yellow Ribbon Program.
