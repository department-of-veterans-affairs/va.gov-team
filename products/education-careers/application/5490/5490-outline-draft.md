# 5490 Application for Education Benefits
Dependent applies for educational transfer of benefits from a deceased or totally disabled servicemember.

## Form outline
-   Applicant information
    - Name
        - First name
        - Middle name
        - Last name
        - Suffix
    - SSN
    - DOB
    - Gender
    - What is your relationship to the Servicemember whose benefit is being transferred to you?
        - Is there a divorce or annulment pending to the qualifying individual?
        - If you are the surviving spouse, have you remarried?
    - _Sponsor's Information_
        - Name
            - First name
            - Middle name
            - Last name
            - Suffix
        - SSN
        - DOB
        - Branch of service
        - Date of death
        - Date listed as MIA or POW
        - Do you or the qualifying individual on whose account you are claiming benefits have an outstanding felony and/or warrant?
        - Is the qualifying individual on active duty?
-   Benefit information
    - Which benefit would you like to use? (_different choices_)
    - Election date
    - Do you expect to receive educational benefits under the Government Employee's Training Act (GETA) for the same course(s) you will receive VA education benefits? (answer only if you are a federal government employee)
      - Source of educational assistance
-   Military History
    -   Have you ever served on active duty in the armed services?
        -   Branch of service
        -   From
        -   To
        -   Type of seperation or discharge
-   Education History
    - Current high school status
        - When did you earn your high school diploma or equivalency certificate?
    - _High school info_
        - Number of years completed
        - From
        - To
        - Name or description of course
        - Name of school
        - Address
        - Number of semester, quarter or clock hours
        - Degree diploma or certificate received
    - Education after high school 
        - Name of college, university or other training provider
        - City
        - State
        - From
        - To
        - Hours
        - Type of hours
        - Degree, diploma or certificate received
        - Major or course of study
-   Employment History
    -   Employer
    -   Main job
    -   Number of months worked
    -   Licenses or rating
-   School selection
    -   Type of education or training
    -   School, university, or training facility
        - Name of school, university, or training facility
        - Address
    -   Education or career goal
    -   The date your training will begin
    -   Are you seeking special restorative training?
    -   Are you seeking special vocational training?
    -   In what state do you plan on living whole participating in this training?
    -   Would you like to receive vocational and educational counseling?
-   Direct deposit
    - Account type
    - Account number
    - Routing number
-   Personal information
    - Address
    - Email address
    - Primary phone
    - Secondary phone
    - How would you prefer to be contacted if VA has questions about your application?
    - Secondary contact
      - First name
      - Last name
      - Address
      - Phone number
-   Review/Submit

**What happens after I apply?**

We usually decide on applications within 30 days.  

You’ll get a Certificate of Eligibility (COE) or decision letter in the mail. If we’ve approved your application, you can bring the COE to the VA certifying official at your school.
