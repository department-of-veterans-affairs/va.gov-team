# Disability Benefit Questionnaire (DBQ) List
DBQs are medical examination forms used to capture essential information for evaluating disability compensation and/or pension claims. There are 71 unique DQBs (as of 10/20/2017).

In order for a claim to be considered and rated properly, the appropriate DBQ must be completed by a medical professional. There are 9 DBQs linked to from VBA that do not load (as of 10/20/2017).

Each DBQ template has an expiration date. If an outdated DBQ is filled out and submitted, the claim will be denied. There are 8 expired DBQs (as of 10/20/2017).

DBQs are maintained by VBA and are publicly accessible [here](https://www.benefits.va.gov/compensation/dbq_ListByDBQFormName.asp)
