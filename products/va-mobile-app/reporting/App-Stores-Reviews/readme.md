# VA Mobile App Store Reviews

Each app review from [Apple](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/va-health-and-benefits/id1559609596) and [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=gov.va.mobileapp&hl=en_US&gl=US&pli=1) stores are analyzed and tagged with keywords that align to a product area or UX. Each keyword category is then analyzed for the trending sentiment (positive, negative, or mixed) and review highlights are included in the summaries.
