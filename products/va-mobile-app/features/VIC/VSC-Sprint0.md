# Sprint 0 Product Brief - Veteran Status Card

[Link to Epic]

## Problem Statement
Veterans do not have an easy way to demonstrate they are a Veteran

### Current Experience ###
Currently there is the VIC card, but it is slow, unmaintained, and not a great UX

### Desired Outcomes ###

Vetarans have a way to quickly demonstrate they are indeed a Veteran anywhere they are:
Easily discoverable after login 
User sees info on how it might be useful 
Personalized with user identification info 
Can add to Apple Wallet/Android equivalent
