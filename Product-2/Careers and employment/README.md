# Careers and employment
Welcome to the Careers and employment hub. In this directory, you'll find lots of information about the Veteran-facing services and applications that fall under the Careers and employment category.

[Check out Careers and employment on VA.gov.](https://www.va.gov/careers-employment/)

Browse through the product folders to find all the details, background, contacts, and status of all the Careers and employment tools. 

Product Managers are responsible for keeping these directories up-to-date and well-organized. If you have a question and can't find the answer, don't hesitate to send a message to the #vfs-product channel in Slack.
