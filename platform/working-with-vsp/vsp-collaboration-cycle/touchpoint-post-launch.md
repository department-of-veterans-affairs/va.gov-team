# Post-launch Check-in

Did we recommend a Post-launch Check-in? If so, follow the below guidelines to get started.

## When should I request this touchpoint?

One month after "Go Live" (as defined in the [Release Plan](https://github.com/department-of-veterans-affairs/va.gov-team/blob/master/platform/product-management/release-plan-template.md))

## What is the collaboration format?

Asynchronous review

## What is the objective of this touchpoint?

Evaluate the success of your product, now that it’s released and you’ve gathered some initial KPI data. Begin your plan for iteration.

## How do I request this touchpoint?

VFS Product Manager messages @Shira Goodman in Slack on [#vfs-all-teams](https://dsva.slack.com/archives/CE4304QPK) channel and links to completed Release Plan.

## What artifacts do I provide?

VFS provides: 
- Completed [Release Plan](https://github.com/department-of-veterans-affairs/va.gov-team/blob/master/platform/product-management/release-plan-template.md) with results populated, including answers written to the Post-launch Questions at the bottom

## What is the outcome of this touchpoint?

Begin thinking about how to iterate and evolve your product based on the results you've reported.
