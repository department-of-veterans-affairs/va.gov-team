# Purpose and Scope

The Privacy Threshold Analysis (PTA) is a tool created by the VA Privacy Service and used by Privacy Officers, Information System Owners, Information System Security Officers (ISSOs), and other stakeholders. Based on the information in the PTA, PIA Support determines the following:

Whether a Privacy Impact Assessment (PIA) is required under the E-Government Act of 2002.
1. Upon completion of an initial review, the VSP ATO Specialist will forward a copy to the VA PIA Support Mail Group for final review.
2) After review the PTA review, the VSP ATO may contact the PM if a PIA may be required.
